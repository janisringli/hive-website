WHAT WE DO
HIVE is a Swisscom internal team of apprentices only. The team of developers and designers produces and designs apps, websites and supports you in many other ways. We promote ideas and want to realize new projects. If someone has an idea, we take care of it and create the possibilities. We are a group that supports Swisscom internal projects with ambition and passion.
Marketing
Marketing is of vital importance to any business. It is the key process of researching, promoting and selling products or services to your target market. Our apprentices can help you to make people aware of your project through social media, blogs, promotional videos and more.
