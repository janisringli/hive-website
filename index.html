WHAT WE DO
HIVE is a Swisscom internal team of apprentices only. The team of developers and designers produces and designs apps, websites and supports you in many other ways. We promote ideas and want to realize new projects. If someone has an idea, we take care of it and create the possibilities. We are a group that supports Swisscom internal projects with ambition and passion.
Pitch-Deck Design
Let our team help you succeed in getting further by designing a pitch deck that conveys your message and convinces sponsors. Our apprentice designers will put together a presentation suitable for the business field. This presentation will be editable afterwards - depending on the feedback, further customization can always be made.
Web Design
A website is your most powerful tool for communication between you and your partners, sponsors and clients. A website is a unique way to connect with the world. Our team of designers and developers will design a mockup for you. Depending on the feedback, changes and improvements will be made, which will then be realized by our team of developers.
Web development
Would you like to increase your networks, share your passion, meet people or introduce your services? It's your decision! After our design team has created a website for you that meets your requirements, our team of developers can create it for you.
Marketing
Marketing is of vital importance to any business. It is the key process of researching, promoting and selling products or services to your target market. Our apprentices can help you to make people aware of your project through social media, blogs, promotional videos and more.
